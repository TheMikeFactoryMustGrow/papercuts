## Papercuts (per-repo only)

Friction capture is **project-scoped**, not global. Data lives in each repo under
`.agent-papercuts/`. Ambient logging applies only when that repo’s `AGENTS.md`
has the papercuts block (`papercut enable` once per project).

Do **not** log to a root `PAPERCUTS.md` or a machine-wide log.

Skills (install once): **papercuts** (sand open list) + **papercuts-kaizen** (long cycle).
Package: https://github.com/TheMikeFactoryMustGrow/papercuts
