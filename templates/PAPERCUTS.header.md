# PAPERCUTS

Agent-logged friction — small things that got in the way while working.
Not a bug tracker. Not a changelog. Just a sanding list (open items only).

Log:   `papercut "what you were doing → what got in the way"`
Clear: `papercut resolve --stamp <ts> -n "what you fixed"`  (writes shadow history)
Sand:  skill **papercuts** · Longer cycle: skill **papercuts-kaizen**
